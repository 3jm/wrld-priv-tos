# Privacy Policy — Wrld Bot

**Last Updated:** June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Wrld ("the Bot") collects and how it is used.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

Wrld may collect and store the following data as necessary to function:

- **User IDs** — to identify users across commands
- **Server (Guild) IDs** — to store server-specific settings
- **Command usage data** — to process and respond to commands
- **Message content** — only when directly required by a command; not logged or stored otherwise

We do **not** collect passwords, emails, or any personal information outside of Discord.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used solely to:
- Operate and improve the Bot's features
- Store user or server preferences/settings

We do **not** sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties.

## 3. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed to provide the Bot's functionality. Server or user data may be deleted upon request or when the Bot is removed from a server.

## 4. Third Parties

Wrld operates on the Discord platform and is subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy). We do not use any other third-party data processors.

## 5. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please contact us through our support server. We will process requests within a reasonable timeframe.

## 6. Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of Wrld after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---

*For questions or data requests, contact us through our support server.*
